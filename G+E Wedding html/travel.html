Location

Anderson Lodge is a Scandinavian resort, nestled in the forest near Mt. St. Helens.
The address is:
Anderson Main Lodge
12025 Lewis River Road · Ariel · WA 98603

Lodging

We've rented out the entire Main Lodge for our family & friends.
The Main Lodge, which includes the Bunkloft, has 43 twin beds and 12 double beds. This includes one private room, family rooms, dorm style rooms, and a bunkloft.The Dalarna Cabin is on the same 16 acre site and has four double beds. There is enough room to accomodate 69 people.

Travel

FLIGHTS:
Making your flight travel plans is probably on the top of your priority list. For that we would recommend flights out of your departure city into Portland, Oregon. Portland International Airport is just a one hour drive from Anderson Lodge.
CAR RENTAL: Once you arrive, you may want to rent a car or van to easily move about the state. You may also want to see about a shuttle, car service or carpool with other guests to Anderson Lodge.
Please let us know if you are interested in carpooling and we'll put you in touch with other interested parties.
